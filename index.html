BAND BIO

The WesBytes are a three piece experimental harsh noise band with a focus on animal biometrics. Their debut album, NOIZE 4 SALE, was recorded at the bottom of the Charles River using state-of-the art underwater recording technology. The music is inspired by the culture of the 1950's, except it's cyber-punk, steam-punk, and Gotham. The trio's main inspiration is pioneering 1950's noise rock artist, Elvis Presley.

ADAM'S BIO
Hailing from Michigan, Adam plays electric digeridoo and the washboard. From the latest album, he composed the tracks "Gotta Have that Noize" and "10LUFS to Heaven". Adam is currently working on new material for his debut solo album, Alone With my Noize. The project will be available for streaming exclusively on LimeWire starting July 6th.
