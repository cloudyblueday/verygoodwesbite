BAND BIO

The WesBytes are a three piece experimental harsh noise band with a focus on animal biometrics. Their debut album, NOIZE 4 SALE, was recorded at the bottom of the Charles River using state-of-the art underwater recording technology.

ADAM'S BIO
Hailing from Michigan, Adam plays electric digeridoo and the washboard. From the latest album, he composed the tracks "Gotta Have that Noize" and "10LUFS to Heaven"
